About Us



About Us

Marudhar education society has been founded by a group of professional, technocrats, Industrialist and social workers. This group has objectives of setting up reasonably good professional institute in the state of Rajasthan. To begin with, they have registered this society with the Registrar of Societies, Bikaner (Rajasthan) vide registration no. 99/2002-03 dated 21 Sept 2002. Immediately after the registration, the society got the permission from AICTE to start an Engineering College at Bikaner. It started Marudhar Engineering College and Swami Keshvanand Institute of Pharmacy at Bikaner from July 2003 & July 2005 respectively. The Swami Keshvanand Institute of Pharmacy successfully entered in to 15 year from July 2019.

Marudhar Education Society has an experience of establishment educational institutes of very good standard. Following institute have been establishment by this group. Higher secondary School at Jaipur and Bikaner Marudhar Engineering College, Bikaner Swami Keshvanand Institute of Pharmacy, Bikaner This society proposes to develop a big educational centre catering to variety of technical course. Hence, as the next step, the society has decided to submit a proposal fro expanding Pharmacy College by adding diploma course. SKIP is a reputed and premier institute for imparting quality education in the field of pharmacy and its emerging dimensions. As a platform for excellence in teaching, learning and administration, advanced information technology is used in contributing to the development of well trained graduates to meet the professional requirements. Education at SKIP is a holistic one which is aimed at developing the intellectual and personal strengths of our students.

We have a community of students and staff dedicated towards bringing out the best in all its members. Our aim is to provide the conducive environment for teaching, learning and research.

At SKIP, we are committed to create a vibrant and stimulating environment both for study, extra-curricular and sports activities, thus ensuring complete personality development of student. We constantly seek in creating the best infrastructure and improve the facilities for the students and staff members.

Students are our most valuable resource. We hope and desire that Pharma graduates of our institute would use their knowledge and competence in making our great nation a global player in the field of pharmacy.
