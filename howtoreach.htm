How to Reach

Flying into Bikaner Airport

The institute is located just 30 minutes away from the Nal airport. If you happen to fly into Bikaner, one could easily take a cab from the airport to get to the institute.



Taking a bus to Bikaner

If you are travelling by a bus to the institute, one could take the bus from Museum circle . The last mile connectivity is provided by tempos and auto rickshaws that ply in abundance from the Raisar Village to the institute. Shared cabs are also available.



Taking a train to Bikaner

If you are travelling into Bikaner by train, one is advised to alight at bikaner Junction. The institute is located approximately 22 km from the station and it may take up to 30 minutes travelling to the institute by a cab. Other suitable railway stations to use in Bikaner include Lalgarh station . However, one is strongly advised to check on train halt at the station as also onward travel arrangements before alighting at the station.
