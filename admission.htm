Admission:

B.Pharm

Eligibility for admission:
Passed 10+2 examination with physics and chemistry as compulsory subjects along with one of the mathematics/biotechnology/biology. Obtained at leat 45% marks(40% in case of candidate belonging to reserved category) in above subjects taken together.

Fee: Rs.51,000/- per annum + Rs. 7,500/- caution money (refundable) The fees structure is as per policy of Govt. of Rajasthan.


Direct admission to B.Pharm-II year through Lateral Entry for D. Pharm holders:

Candidate should have aggregate of 50% marks in D.Pharm



* Separate Hostel accommodation facility available for boys and girls within campus
* Boys(Single Seater)- Rs.55000/- + Rs. 7500/-(Security Money)


* Girls(Single Seater)- Rs.60000/- + Rs. 7500/-(Security Money)


* (Double Seater)- Rs.55000/- + Rs. 7500/-(Security Money)
